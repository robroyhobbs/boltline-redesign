Our Story

Built by engineers who build rockets.

Boltline started inside Stoke Space. When rocket engineers couldn't find software fast enough for their hardware, they built their own. They needed something that could track 10,000+ parts across design, build, test, and trace -- without six months of implementation or a team of consultants. So they created it from scratch, on the factory floor, under the pressure of real launch schedules. Now it's yours.
