Our Story

We built this because we needed it.

Boltline started inside Stoke Space, where engineers were building reusable rockets and couldn't find software that kept up. Every existing tool was either a spreadsheet held together with hope, a legacy PLM system that cost more than the hardware, or a patchwork of disconnected apps that nobody trusted.

So they built their own. After proving it on actual rocket hardware — managing thousands of parts across prototyping, qualification, and production — they made it available to every hardware team that refuses to accept "that's just how it is."
